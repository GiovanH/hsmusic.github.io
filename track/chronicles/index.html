Chronicles

By Tyler Dever and Erik Scheele.
Released 7/13/2011.

Listen on YouTube.

Tracks that Chronicles references:

  • Candles and Clockwork by Tyler Dever

Artist commentary:

Tyler Dever:

A chronicle is an account of chronological events. Homestuck does not follow a chronological structure in theslightest. Chronicles is a piano version of Candles and Clockwork. Candles and Clockwork in itself is a massivehemiola as each voice exists in a different time signature. The melody is adapted in this piece to match the 3/4arpeggio. The piece keeps changing to reflect each leg of the journey the kids have to experience in order tofinish the game, not necessarily in order.
