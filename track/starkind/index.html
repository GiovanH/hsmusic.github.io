Starkind

By Toby Fox.
Cover art by Brad Griffin.
Released 2011-07-30.

Listen on Bandcamp.

Tracks that reference Starkind:

Fandom:
  • Emissary of Dance by Yishan Mai

Artist commentary:

Toby Fox:

I saw the updates for Rose blowing stuff up and finding that giant Captchalogue card with the Green Sun on it, and then someone said "is that a strife specibus card? What would that be? Starkind?" or something to that effect. And that inpsired me ot make a pretty awesome boss song that was too much like other things I've made to put on an album.

Brad Griffin:

Originally this picture didn't have Rose in it at all. It was just a generic silhouette standing, holding a star on a specibus card. That was kinda lame, and dind't really fit the feel of the song. I think I improved it.
